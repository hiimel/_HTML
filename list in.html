Kachchi Biryani

Ingredients:

  1. Basmati rice - 500 grams
  2. Khassi meat -1kg
  3. Yogrut - 200 grams
  4. Onions - 4
  5. Ginger-garlic paste
  6. Hot spices

Cooking steps:

  1. Wash the rice and soak it for 30 minutes
  2. Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices for 2 hours
  3. Fry the chopped onion until brown
  4. Arrange the meat in the pot
  5. Place the wet rice on the top of the meats
  6. Spread the saffron-infused milk
  7. Simmer with a lid(45 minutes)
  8. Take it out and serve it hot

Total time: 2 hours
